Our story

Black T-Shirt was born in Ouarzazate, Morocco — a city known for its film sets, light and energy. We wanted to create something just as iconic: a simple, powerful black T-shirt that fits perfectly into modern Moroccan life.

Instead of chasing every trend, we focus on one product and make it right. Clean lines, premium fabric and a fit that works on the streets, in class, at work or on a night out.

What we stand for

  • Minimalism — One essential piece that works with everything you own.
  • Quality — Comfortable fabric, sharp color and a fit tested on real people.
  • Accessibility — Premium feel without the heavy price tag. Every tee is 200 MAD.
  • Moroccan street culture — Designed with the rhythm of our streets, campuses and cafés in mind.
